The Million Pound Drop!

Objective:

  • The player starts with £1,000,000 in the bank and must answer 10 multiple choice questions. Your aim is to keep as much money as possible.

Questions:

  • Each round presents contestants with a question and four possible answers.
  • Contestants must decide which answer they believe to be correct.

Money Allocation:

  • Contestants start the game with a total of one million pounds.
  • Contestents distribute this money among the answer choices by placing it on trapdoors corresponding to their chosen answers.
  • Money can be distributed unevenly or placed entirely on one answer.

Time Limit:

  • Contestants have a 30 seconds to allocate their money before incorrect trapdoors open, causing any money placed on them to be lost.

Drop Zone:

  • Money placed on trapdoors corresponding to incorrect answers falls through a "drop zone" and is lost from the game.

Correct Answer Reveal:

  • After incorrect trapdoors drop, the correct answer is revealed.
  • Any money placed on correct answers is retained by the contestants.

Elimination:

  • Contestants are eliminated if they lose all their money in any round.
